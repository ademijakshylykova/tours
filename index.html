About Us

About Us Welcome to At-Bashy Travel! With over 20 years of expertise, we specialize in crafting unforgettable journeys across the breathtaking landscapes of Kyrgyzstan. Our tours, ranging from exhilarating horse treks and scenic hikes to customized car tours, are designed to immerse travelers in the authentic beauty and rich culture of our homeland. As locals, we take pride in sharing the hidden gems of Kyrgyzstan, from the towering peaks of the Tien Shan mountains to serene alpine lakes and vast green valleys. Our experienced guides bring a deep knowledge of the region, ensuring that each tour is not only safe and comfortable but also filled with enriching stories, traditions, and unique encounters with local communities. Whether you’re seeking an adventurous trek through untouched wilderness, a cultural journey to remote villages, or simply a peaceful retreat in nature, At-Bashy Travel is here to help you experience Kyrgyzstan in a way you’ll never forget. Join us for a journey of a lifetime—let us show you the true spirit of our land!
